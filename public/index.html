Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need coding skills?

No. Just paste your URL and copy one line of code to embed. That's it. No technical knowledge required.

How does the AI learn my website?

We automatically scan your website pages and train the AI on your content, products and services. It becomes an expert on your business.

What languages does it support?

The AI automatically detects the customer's language and responds accordingly. Supports Arabic, French, English, Spanish and more.

Can I add information manually?

Yes. In your dashboard you can paste any extra info like pricing, FAQs or promotions that aren't on your website.

Is there a free plan?

Yes. Free forever with 1 chatbot and 50 messages/month. No credit card required. No time limit.

What happens if I exceed the free limit?

Your chatbot will pause until the next month or you can upgrade to Pro for unlimited access when it's available.
